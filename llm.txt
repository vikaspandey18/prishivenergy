# Prishiv Energy Private Limited

> Powering Progress, Delivering Trust

Prishiv Energy Private Limited is a premium supplier and turnkey solutions partner for heavy-duty industrial power infrastructure. Serving major builders, continuous processing industries, and government infrastructure projects throughout India.

## Core Offerings

### 1. Diesel Generator Sets (DG Sets)
* **Capacity Range:** 5 kVA to 3000 kVA
* **Engines:** Cummins, Kirloskar, Perkins, Caterpillar
* **Alternators:** Stamford, Leroy Somer (H-Class, copper wound)
* **Compliance:** CPCB IV+ emission certified, soundproof enclosures (<75 dBA at 1m)
* **Controls:** AMF Panels & Multi-generator synchronization grid design

### 2. Distribution & Power Transformers
* **Capacity Range:** Up to 50 MVA, 132kV class
* **Types:** Oil-immersed (ONAN/ONAF) and dry-type (cast resin/vacuum impregnated)
* **Materials:** Electrolytic grade copper windings, CRGO steel cores (BEE Star Rated)
* **Options:** Low-loss Amorphous core transformers (reduces no-load loss by 70%)

### 3. Servo Voltage Stabilizers
* **Capacity Range:** 10 kVA to 2000 kVA
* **Models:** Air-cooled (up to 150 kVA) and Oil-cooled (up to 2000 kVA)
* **Precision:** Correction speed >35V/sec, output regulation ±0.5%
* **Applications:** Protecting MRI machines, CNC processing lines, printing presses, and server grids

### 4. Industrial Pump Systems
* **Models:** Split-case centrifugal pumps, multi-stage inline booster pumps, borewell submersibles
* **Motors:** IE3 Premium Efficiency motors equipped with VFD (Variable Frequency Drive) smart controls
* **Networks:** Fire fighting networks, municipal grids, and real estate booster networks
